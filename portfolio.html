Coverage & Installation FAQs

You can call our office directly or use the "Check Availability" button on this page. Our team will verify coverage for your exact location and get back to you within 24 hours. We cover Kadayanallur, Tenkasi, and surrounding villages.
For areas with existing infrastructure, installation is typically completed within 1 working day. For new fibre line runs, it may take 2–3 days depending on distance and site conditions. We always aim for same-day activation once the line is installed.
